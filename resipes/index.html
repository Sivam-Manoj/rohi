hydrabad Briyani

Instructions:
1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 1-2 hours.
2 In a large pot, heat oil or ghee. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions for garnishing.
3 Add ginger-garlic paste to the remaining onions in the pot and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
4 Add chopped tomatoes, biryani masala, and green chilies. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
5 Add marinated chicken to the pot and cook until the chicken is partially cooked.
6 Meanwhile, parboil the soaked basmati rice with some whole spices until it's 70-80% cooked. Drain the water.
7 Layer the partially cooked chicken with mint leaves, coriander leaves, and half of the fried onions.
8 Top the chicken layer with the parboiled rice. Sprinkle some biryani masala, mint leaves, coriander leaves, and the remaining fried onions.
9 Mix saffron strands in warm milk and drizzle it over the rice for a fragrant and colorful touch.
10 Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or seal it with dough to trap the steam.
11 Cook on low heat for about 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked, and the rice is tender.
12 Gently fluff the biryani with a fork, ensuring not to break the rice grains.
13 Serve the flavorful Hyderabad Chicken Biryani hot with raita or a side salad. Enjoy your delicious homemade biryani!

    ingridients :
  • 1 kg chicken, cleaned and cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogur
  • 4 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 bunch fresh mint leaves
  • 1 bunch fresh coriander leaves
  • 4-5 green chilies, chopped
  • Whole spices (bay leaves, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves)
  • Salt to taste
    For Marination:
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

Crispy Fried Chicken

Instructions:
1 . Marinate the Chicken:
Place the chicken pieces in a bowl. Pour enough buttermilk over the chicken to completely submerge it. Cover the bowl and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight. The acidity in the buttermilk helps tenderize the chicken.
2. Prepare the Coating:
In a separate bowl, mix the all-purpose flour, cornstarch, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper (if using). Adjust the seasonings to taste.
3. Coat the Chicken:
Heat the oil in a deep fryer or a large, deep pan to around 350-375°F (175-190°C). Take each piece of chicken from the buttermilk, letting excess drip off, and coat it thoroughly with the flour mixture. Press the flour mixture onto the chicken to ensure a crispy coating.
4. Fry the Chicken:
Carefully place the coated chicken into the hot oil. Fry a few pieces at a time to avoid overcrowding the pan. Fry until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through. The internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C). Remove the fried chicken from the oil and place it on a wire rack to drain excess oil.
5. Enjoy:
Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute, keeping the chicken moist. Serve your crispy fried chicken with your favorite sides, like coleslaw, mashed potatoes, or a dipping sauce. Enjoy your delicious homemade fried chicken

Chocholate Cake

Instructions:
1. For the Cake:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
In a large mixing bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda.
Add sugar, eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
Gradually add boiling water to the batter, mixing continuously. The batter will be thin, but that's okay.
Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

2. For the Frosting:

In a large bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.
Sift in cocoa powder and add powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract. Beat until smooth and creamy.
If the frosting is too thick, you can add more milk; if it's too thin, add more powdered sugar.

3. Assembling the Cake:

Once the cakes are completely cooled, place one cake layer on a serving plate.
Spread a layer of chocolate frosting on top of the first layer.
Place the second cake layer on top and frost the top and sides of the entire cake with the remaining frosting.
You can decorate the cake with additional chocolate shavings, sprinkles, or berries if desired.
Slice and enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate cake!

    ingridients :
  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
    For the Frosting:
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Crispy Samosa

Instructions:
1. Prepare the Filling:
Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
Add grated ginger, minced green chilies, and sauté for a minute.
Add turmeric powder, ground coriander, garam masala, and sauté for another minute.
Add boiled and mashed potatoes, boiled peas, amchur, salt, and mix well.
Cook the mixture for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add chopped fresh coriander leaves, mix, and let the filling cool.
2. Make the Dough:
In a bowl, mix the all-purpose flour, salt, and oil or ghee.
Gradually add water and knead to form a smooth, firm dough.
Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
3. Assemble and Fry the Samosas:

Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each portion into a ball.
Roll each ball into a thin oval or round shape.
Cut the rolled dough in half to form two semi-circles.
Take one semi-circle, create a cone shape, and seal the edges with a water-flour paste to form a cone.
Fill the cone with the prepared potato-peas filling and seal the open edge to form a triangular shape.
Heat oil for deep frying in a pan.
Fry the samosas on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy.
Remove the samosas and place them on paper towels to absorb excess oil.
Serve the crispy samosas with mint chutney or tamarind sauce. Enjoy your delicious homemade samosas!

    ingridients :
  • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup boiled and roughly mashed peas
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon minced green chilies (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
    For the Dough:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup oil or ghee
  • Water for kneading
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
